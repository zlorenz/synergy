Life Insurance

Whole Life

Also known as ordinary life insurance, Whole Life is a permanent life insurance policy that covers an individual until his or her death while accumulating a cash value within the policy.

Term Life

Term Life insurance is a temporary life insurance policy that covers an individual for a specific number of years (generally 10, 20 or 30 years) and then coverage ends.

Universal Life

Also known as adjustable life insurance, Universal Life is a life insurance policy that offers the low cost features of Term Life insurance with the cash value concept of Whole Life insurance. Universal Life insurance may also offer more flexible premium options than Ordinary or Whole Life insurance.
